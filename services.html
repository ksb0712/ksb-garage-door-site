Professional Services

Garage Door Repair

We diagnose the root cause, not just the symptom. From misaligned tracks and worn rollers to damaged panels and noisy hinges — every repair is finished with a safety reversal test and balance check.

LiftMaster Motors & Smart Openers

Quiet belt‑drive openers with Wi‑Fi (myQ), battery backup and modern safety sensors. Keypads and remote programming included, with force and travel limits calibrated correctly.

Springs & Cables

Precision torsion/extension spring sizing and installation. We wind to spec, set correct balance and replace frayed cables to protect your opener and reduce noise.

New Door Installation

Insulated steel, carriage‑house and modern flush designs. We remove the old door, install premium hardware, balance the system and complete a full quality inspection.
